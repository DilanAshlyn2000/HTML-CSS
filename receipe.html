BREW MIST LATTE

INGREDIENTS:

2 cups milk

1 â…“ cups hot freshly brewed dark roast espresso coffee

Directions:

STEP 1: Heat milk in a saucepan set over medium-low heat.

STEP 2: Whisk briskly with a wire whisk to create foam.

STEP 3: Brew espresso and pour into four cups.

STEP 4: Pour in milk, holding back the foam with a spoon. Spoon foam over the top.
